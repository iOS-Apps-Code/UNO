Your Rights

- You can view, correct, update, restrict the processing of, or request the transfer or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us at dataprivacy@mattel163.com.

- You can reset your mobile ad ID (IDFA or AAID) at any time through your mobile device settings, which will allow you to limit the use of information collected about you. You may also visit http://www.aboutads.info/appchoices.

- The location data collected through an app depends on your device settings and app permissions. You can exercise choice over the location data collected through our apps by disabling: location permissions on the app or device level and Bluetooth and WiFi scanning.

Special Notice Regarding Children

- Mattel163 and/or our third-party service providers collect persistent identifiers necessary for the support of our internal operations. However, we and/or our third-party service providers disable all other collection of personal information from children under the age of consent (i.e., 13 in the US), except when necessary to provide a one-time response to a child’s request (i.e., customer service).

- Parents or guardians of a child under the age of consent have the right to contact us to review, correct, or delete their child’s data at any time, or to refuse to permit further collection or use of their child’s data, by emailing us at dataprivacy@mattel163.com.

Special Notice Regarding U.S. Residents

However, if you are a U.S. resident and you have any questions about our disclosure of your personal information to third parties, please contact us at dataprivacy@mattel163.com.
